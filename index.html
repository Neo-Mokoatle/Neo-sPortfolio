  • Project 1: IEC online voting system

    The Online Voting System aims to revolutionize the traditional voting process by introducing a secure, efficient, and user-friendly online platform for national elections. This system will provide a modern alternative to the pen-and-paper voting method, addressing issues such as manual vote counting, long queues, voter fraud, and the complexity of choosing from numerous candidates. By leveraging technology, the system seeks to enhance the electoral process's transparency, accuracy, and accessibility. Objectives *Modernization: Replace the traditional voting method with an advanced online platform. *Efficiency: Streamline the voting process, reducing the time taken to cast and count votes. *Security: Implement robust security measures to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of votes. *Accessibility: Make the voting process more accessible to all eligible voters, regardless of their physical location. *Transparency: Provide real-time updates and clear, transparent results The project encompasses the development, testing, deployment, and maintenance of the Online Voting System..
